The Gem

A Hotel in Downtown Montreal

In collaboration with Aly Stein

Hotels have a great potential as cultural centres due to a constant flow of people from all around the world. The Gem allows for a rare coexistence of communal and corporate spaces in a hotel. The design ...aims to cater to the need for functionality and comfort all while providing spaces that allow for unexpected encounters and interactions between tourists and locals. To achieve this, The Gem’s program needed to diverge from the program of typical hotels.

Traditionally, a hotel’s ground floor is planned for lounging and commerce. However, the design is centered around zones of activities: communal gardens, a fitness centre and a sculpture studio. In addition, co-working spaces and rentable offices on the last three floors of the building promote innovation and the sharing of ideas. The project responds to the current needs of the quartier all while foreseeing its evolution.
