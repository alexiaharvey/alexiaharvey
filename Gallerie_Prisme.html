Gallerie Prisme

An art gallery in Montreal's Mile End

A gallery must have a symbiotic relationship with the art it houses. It must have its own creative presence all while responding to the site and the neighborhoodâ€™s social context. ... The PRISME gallery allows visitors to learn about art while learning about themselves.

This projet is conceived to entice the senses of users and to create a fluid promenade from one room to another. The works of art are displayed in spaces that allow for the filtration of natural light. The exhibition rooms are modest, minimal and pure, which can allow for the coexistence of stimulating spaces and meditative ones. The works of art will give the space distinctive smells, unique sounds and colorful lighting.
